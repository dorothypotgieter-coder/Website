Day 3 of 17 · Sightseeing

Exploring Batumi

Monday, 15 June
Busy day. Woke up just before 10, so overslept we almost didn't make breakfast! Then we began exploring the city — first the Euphoria Hotel next door, where the girls will compete, then a little mall, then decided to just walk the city. At a point we both needed the bathroom and didn't know where to find public facilities, so I looked for somewhere to have a beer and a cooldrink to enable a bathroom break, and stumbled onto one of the best Georgian restaurants in the process — with Adjarian Khachapuri so delicious that Evan declared the bread (he only ate the ends) better than chocolate cake, and anything he's eaten (only the caramel Charlotte cake still beats it). The pear lemonade was like drinking a pear — just divine. We walked over a bridge onto a small island on the little lake next to the restaurant, and a local told us to turn right and walk twenty minutes for something beautiful. Blisters and over an hour later, but worth it — we found the Alphabet Tower, the Ali and Nino statue (they move with the sea, so never in the same position), a big wheel, and a Jack Sparrow lookalike boat on the Black Sea. A pretty amazing day. With the sun up so late we lost track of time and only made it back to the hotel just before 9, right at the end of supper.— Simone
